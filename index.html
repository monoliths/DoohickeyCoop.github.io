Welcome to the Doohickey Co-op. Please pardon the look and brief message while we update our website.

The Doohickey Co-op is a convenient, innovative, community-owned resource created to facilitate the sharing of hand and power tools for personal use and to provide a collaborative workspace to help members bring their ideas to life.

Through the dedication of our volunteers, a clear mission, grounded values, and the vision of our directors and advisors, the Doohickey Co-op is positioned to set a new standard for community-owned tool libraries and makerspaces.
